About Atlas

Search inside video subtitles, then jump to the exact spoken moment.

Atlas is a subtitle-first retrieval prototype for long-form YouTube video. It helps users move from a remembered phrase or topic to the second that actually matters.

Subtitle-first retrieval
Source-aware search
Moment-level navigation

What Atlas does

It finds the moment, not just the video.

Instead of relying only on video titles or channel metadata, Atlas searches timestamped subtitle segments, groups nearby hits, and turns them into navigable entry points.

The result is a cleaner workflow for finding spoken evidence, revisiting useful clips, and moving through long videos with much less friction.
